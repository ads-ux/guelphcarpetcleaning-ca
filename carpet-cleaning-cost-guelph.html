How Much Does Carpet Cleaning Cost in Guelph?

Published 2026-06-01 — Guelph Carpet Cleaning

Professional carpet cleaning in Guelph is priced per room or per square foot, depending on the company. Here's what to expect for common service types in 2026.

Pricing by Room Size

  • Standard bedroom (120–150 sq ft): $120–$200
  • Living room (250–350 sq ft): $180–$280
  • Full main floor (600–900 sq ft of carpet): $350–$600
  • Whole home (3 bedrooms + living spaces): $450–$800
  • Stairs: $4–$8 per step

Pricing by Method

Truck-mounted hot water extraction (steam cleaning) is the industry standard and what most professional companies in Guelph use. It's the most thorough method and the one carpet manufacturers recommend to maintain warranties. This is what the prices above reflect.

Portable machine cleaning is less expensive but significantly less effective — the water pressure and heat are lower, meaning less soil extraction and longer dry times. Be cautious of very low-priced services; they often use portable equipment.

Dry cleaning / low-moisture methods are faster drying but less thorough. Appropriate for commercial settings or situations where quick dry time is critical.

Add-On Services

  • Pet stain and odour treatment: $30–$60 per room — enzyme treatments that break down urine proteins
  • Scotchgard / protector application: $40–$80 per room — extends time between cleanings
  • Carpet deodorizer: Usually included; ask if not
  • Furniture moving: Some companies include moving standard furniture; heavy pieces (pianos, shelving units) are typically excluded

What Drives the Price Up

  • Heavy soiling requiring multiple passes
  • Pet urine (requires specialized enzyme treatment, not just extraction)
  • Large area rugs that need to be moved for cleaning underneath
  • Deep pile carpet (takes longer and more water to clean thoroughly)

Getting an Accurate Quote

Ask for pricing based on actual square footage measured in your home, not a per-room flat rate — rooms vary significantly in size. Request that the quote include all the specific rooms you want cleaned, and ask whether the price changes if the carpet is heavily soiled or has pet issues.
